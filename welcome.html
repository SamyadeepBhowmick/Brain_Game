Let's Play a Memory Game

    Rules

  • This is a game where one has to enter the limit.
  • According to the limit, a sequence of numbers will be generated between 0 and 9.
  • We have to memorise the sequence before it disappears in 5 seconds.
  • We will insert the correct sequence one by one in the pop up box.
  • An aleat will be triggered to inform whether the input sequence is correct.
